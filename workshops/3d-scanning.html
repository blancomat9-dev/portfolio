3D Scanning

Overview

Converting physical objects into digital geometry using structured-light scanning, taught with an Artec Leo 3D scanner that generates point clouds refined into single optimized meshes.

Covered

  • How structured-light scanning operates
  • Techniques for capturing clean scanning data
  • Processing and aligning scan data
  • Optimizing scans for CAD or manufacturing applications

Notes

A common-issues chart helped students plan how to prepare objects for scanning (reflective, dark, or transparent surfaces need treatment before capture).
