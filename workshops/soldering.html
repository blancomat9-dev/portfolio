Soldering

Overview

An electronics soldering workshop focused on creating reliable, permanent electrical connections, guiding students through proper technique, flux behavior, joint anatomy, and safe equipment handling while assembling functional hardware.

Covered

  • Why proper connections matter, and multiple methods to achieve them
  • What soldering is and how it works
  • Recognizing all the soldering outcomes, and what a properly soldered pin looks like
  • Assembling a working circuit as a hands-on exercise

Focus

Craftsmanship, rework strategies, and building durable, serviceable electronics.
