Arduino 1

Overview

Microcontroller fundamentals focused on connecting code to physical systems, where participants learned how microcontrollers interact with sensors and actuators through hands-on wiring and programming.

Covered

  • Digital and analog inputs and outputs
  • Algorithmic thinking and debugging
  • Building intuition for embedded systems

Activities

  • Intro to what an Arduino is and the kinds of projects it enables
  • Troubleshooting simple circuits to get Arduinos working
  • Using analog inputs to measure current and build an ammeter
  • Combining digital and analog outputs to build a night light
