Material Testing

Overview

A materials testing workshop focused on experimentally characterizing mechanical behavior, covering specimen preparation, tensile and compression testing, and data analysis to generate stress-strain curves.

Covered

  • Operating a universal testing machine
  • Using software to control the machine and visualize stress-strain curves
  • Breaking composite materials during tests to demonstrate material failure
