ECAD

Overview

A PCB design workshop introducing students to translating functional schematics into manufacturable circuit boards using Altium Designer.

Covered

  • Schematic discipline and design standards for consistency across a board
  • Layout strategy and routing constraints
  • Preparing fabrication-ready outputs and exporting files for manufacturing
  • The types of PCB manufacturing processes available in the lab

Outcome

Students leave with a functional PCB design ready for manufacturing.
